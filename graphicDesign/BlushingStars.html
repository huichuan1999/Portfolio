Blushing Stars


2021

Graphics and illustrations for Art Healing Project for Children with Leukemia

Children's picture books and designs in collaboration with Central Academy of Fine Arts(CAFA) Charity Society and heARTsmove, a social work development center in Beijing.

This picture book is designed to help children who are undergoing long-term medical treatment integrate into school and society. The story features two children: one named Red, representing a child with leukemia, and another named White, representing a healthy child. After receiving treatment, Red's color fades to pink, and White also turns pink when she occasionally gets sick. In the end, they realize that they are not so different from each other; everyone is the same.
